About Me

I worked for Potomac Electric Corporation as an Electronics Engineer where I was involved in the motor manufacturing industry and the production of control drives and power amplifiers starting from PCBs. I build h-bridge rectifiers used for three phase servo motors and power supplies with AC-DC and DC-DC converters using rectifiers and transformers. I got to work with linear encoders and built software for aligning motor resolvers.

Prior to that, I worked for Canvas GFX as a Systems Engineering Technical Support where I created 30+ video tutorials on how to use the Envision software. I worked with 3d cad models in softwares such as SolidWorks and Fusion 360 to edit existing models and create new ones.

My personal interests include arduino/raspbarry pi projects. I play a lot of soccer and volleyball and I am a huge fan of swimming. I recently found out I am also a huge fan of hiking. The biggest hike of mine yet has been Mount. Lafayette through the Falling Waters Trail that took 8 hrs to complete.
